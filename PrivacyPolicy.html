Privacy Policy

Welcome to Jhola! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect ⬇

  • Personal details such as name, email, and contact information.
  • Usage data, IP address, and browsing behavior.
  • Payment and transaction history.
  • Device and browser information.

2. How We Use Your Information ⬇

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • For customer support and communication.
  • To enhance user experience and website security.
  • For legal and regulatory compliance.

3. Cookies and Tracking ⬇

  • We use cookies to improve website functionality.
  • Tracking technologies help us analyze user interactions.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Data Protection ⬇

  • We implement security measures to safeguard your data.
  • Data encryption is used where applicable.
  • Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel.

5. Third-Party Services ⬇

  • We may share data with trusted third parties.
  • Third-party services help with analytics and advertising.
  • We ensure compliance with data privacy regulations.

6. User Rights ⬇

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Request data portability or processing restrictions.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection.

7. Data Retention ⬇

  • We retain data as long as necessary for legal and business purposes.
  • Users can request data deletion.

8. Children's Privacy ⬇

  • We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13.
  • Parents can request removal of children's data.

9. International Data Transfers ⬇

  • Data may be stored or processed outside your country.
  • We use industry-standard safeguards for international transfers.

10. Marketing and Communication ⬇

  • We may send you promotional emails with your consent.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications.

11. Security Breaches ⬇

  • We notify affected users in case of data breaches.
  • Security measures are regularly updated.

12. Legal Compliance ⬇

  • We comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

13. Changes to Privacy Policy ⬇

Jhola reserves the right to update this policy. We encourage you to review it regularly.
