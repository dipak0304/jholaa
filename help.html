Help Center

Find answers to your questions or contact us for further assistance.

Account
How do I create an account?
Go to the sign-up page, fill in your details, and click "Create Account".
How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.
How can I update my profile information?
Go to "My Profile" and edit your details.
How do I delete my account?
Contact customer support to request account deletion.
Orders & Payments
How do I update my address book?
Go to "My Profile" > "Address Book" and update your saved addresses.
What payment options do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery.
Can I change my payment method after placing an order?
No, payment methods cannot be changed after placing an order.
How do I apply a coupon code?
Enter your coupon code at checkout in the discount field.
Shipping & Delivery
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by location, typically 3-7 business days.
Can I track my order?
Yes, go to "My Orders" to track your order.
What if my package is delayed?
Contact customer support for assistance.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we only ship within Nepal.
Returns & Refunds
How do I return an item?
Go to "My Orders" and request a return.
How long do refunds take?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
Can I exchange a product?
Yes, exchanges are available for eligible products.
What items are non-refundable?
Perishable items and digital goods are non-refundable.
